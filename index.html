About EVAN

Built to bring dependable, multispeciality medicine to Muzaffarnagar — without sending families out of town for serious care.

EVAN Multispeciality Hospital & Research Centre is a 100+ bed facility on Bhopa Road, Muzaffarnagar, offering coordinated care across general medicine, surgery, orthopaedics, cardiology, gynaecology, oncology and more — backed by an intensive care unit, in-house diagnostics and a 24/7 emergency department.

The hospital is led by Mr. Vijay Jain (CEO) and Mrs. Manju Jain (Director), with clinical operations headed by Dr. Pankaj Jain, Medical Director. NABH and ISO certification underpin our approach to safety, hygiene and consistent clinical quality — and cashless insurance, Ayushman Bharat and ESIC empanelment keep care accessible.
